Just a little bit about me...

I am an energetic, hard driven individual who loves design, art, psychology, and technology. I am passionate about improving the experience in products and services that users use. It is what makes designing more challenging and fun.

Where I am from...

Born and raised at the heart of Los Angeles, Southern California, I recently want to take my next adventures and challenges of UX in Boston, MA. I went to California State University of Los Angeles and studied Graphic Design | Visual Communications, B.A. I had entered in a M.E.S.A web development competition in high school, won Third place and that's when I got into coding. After I graduated, I went to study web development on my own. I fell in love with UX after my first job in the ecommerce business. I learned a lot from my enthusiastic and amazing mentor Sharon Torres, the former Experience Director of Kelley Blue Book.
